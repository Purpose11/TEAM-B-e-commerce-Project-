Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can enter this tracking number on our website's "Track Order" page to get real-time updates on the status and location of your package.
What is your return policy?
We have a hassle-free return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of delivery. Please ensure that the item is in its original condition and packaging. To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team.
How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team by emailing us at netMart@gmail.com or by calling our toll-free number, which is available on our website's "Contact Us" page. Our dedicated team is available to assist you with any queries or concerns you may have.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. During the checkout process, you can enter your shipping address, and if your country is eligible for shipping, the available shipping options will be displayed along with the associated costs.
Can I cancel or modify my order?
We strive to process orders quickly; however, if you need to cancel or modify your order, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but please note that once the order has been shipped, it cannot be canceled or modified.
Can I pay using multiple payment methods for a single order?
Currently, we support payment using a single method per order. During the checkout process, you can choose your preferred payment method from the available options, such as credit card, PayPal, or Apple Pay. If you wish to use multiple payment methods, we recommend splitting your purchases into separate orders and selecting the appropriate payment method for each order. This ensures a smooth and secure transaction process for both you and our system.
